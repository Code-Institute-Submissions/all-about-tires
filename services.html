Tire Change

We provide with tire swap and balancing on site. You can sit in the cafereria and take hot cup of tea or coffee while we do the services. If you can't wait for the car to be ready, we can provide you with a renthal car for two days at a cost of 20 euro per day.

Tire Hotel

We provide with tire hotel for your winter or summer tires, before they been put on shelfs. We wash them and look for wear and defects. Car renthal is included for customers who have tire hotel at our shop for up to two days.

Customer service
